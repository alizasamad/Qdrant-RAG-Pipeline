He is best known for playing Gary Preston in the sitcom Miranda ( 2009 -- 2015 ) and Lucifer Morningstar in the television series Lucifer ( 2016 -- ) . </P> <H2> Contents </H2> <Ul> <Li> 1 Early life </Li> <Li> 2 Career </Li> <Li> 3 Personal life </Li> <Li> 4 Filmography </Li> <Li> 5 References </Li> <Li> 6 External links </Li> </Ul> <H2> Early life ( edit ) </H2> <P> Ellis was born in Cardiff , the son of Marilyn Jean ( Hooper ) and Christopher John Ellis . His father , sister and uncle are all Baptist ministers , with his uncle , Robert Ellis , being principal of Regent 's Park College , Oxford , which his father also attended . Ellis attended High Storrs School in Sheffield during his education and was a French horn player in the City of Sheffield Youth Orchestra . Tom went on to study BA Dramatic Studies at the Royal Conservatoire of Scotland , which was previously named Royal Scottish Academy of Music and Drama ( RSAMD ) . </P> <H2> Career ( edit ) </H2> <P> Other notable roles include Gary Preston in a hit TV show Miranda aired on BBC co-side with Miranda Hart from November 9 , 2009 to January 1 , 2015 , Justyn in Channel 4 's No Angels and Thomas Milligan in the series three finale of the BBC One science - fiction television programme Doctor Who . In July and August 2009 , Ellis starred in ITV comedy drama Monday Monday with Fay Ripley . He was also cast as Detective Inspector Bland in Agatha Christie 's Poirot . Ellis was the star of the USA network series Rush , playing a Hollywood physician . In February 2015 , it was announced that Ellis was cast as Lucifer Morningstar in the Fox Television drama Lucifer , based on the comic with the same name , which premiered on 25 January 2016 . </P> <H2> Personal life ( edit ) </H2> <P> In 2005 , Estelle Morgan , Ellis ' ex-girlfriend , gave birth to Ellis ' first daughter , Nora . Ellis was married to actress Tamzin Outhwaite from 2006 to 2014 . On 25 June 2008 , she gave birth to their first daughter , Florence Elsie . On 20 December 2008 , the pair appeared on the ITV gameshow All Star Mr & Mrs Christmas Special . Their second daughter , Marnie Mae , was born on 2 August 2012 . On 29 August 2013 , a spokesperson for the couple stated that they had separated , and the following month Outhwaite filed for divorce . </P> <P> As of 2018 , Ellis is engaged to television producer Meaghan Oppenheimer . </P> <H2> Filmography ( edit ) </H2> <Table> Film and television roles <Tr> <Th> Year </Th> <Th> Title </Th> <Th> Role </Th> <Th> Notes </Th> </Tr> <Tr> <Td> </Td> <Td> The Life and Adventures of Nicholas Nickleby </Td> <Td> John Browdie </Td> <Td> </Td> </Tr> <Tr> <Td> High Heels and Low Lifes </Td> <Td> Police Officer </Td> <Td> </Td> </Tr> <Tr> <Td> Buffalo Soldiers </Td> <Td> Squash </Td> <Td> </Td> </Tr> <Tr> <Td> 2001 -- 02 </Td> <Td> Nice Guy Eddie </Td> <Td> Frank Bennett </Td> <Td> </Td> </Tr> <Tr> <Td> 2003 </Td> <Td> Pollyanna </Td> <Td> Timothy </Td> <Td> </Td> </Tr> <Tr> <Td> I 'll Be There </Td> <Td> Ivor </Td> <Td> </Td> </Tr> <Tr> <Td> </Td> <Td> Messiah III : The Promise </Td> <Td> Dr. Phillip Ryder </Td> <Td> </Td> </Tr> <Tr> <Td> Vera Drake </Td> <Td> Police Officer </Td> <Td> </Td> </Tr> <Tr> <Td> 2005 </Td> <Td> Much Ado About Nothing </Td> <Td> Claudio </Td> <Td> </Td> </Tr> <Tr> <Td> Midsomer Murders </Td> <Td> Lee Smeeton </Td> <Td> Episode : `` Midsomer Rhapsody '' </Td> </Tr> <Tr> <Td> Waking the Dead </Td> <Td> Harry Taylor </Td> <Td> Episode : `` Straw Dog '' </Td> </Tr> <Tr> <Td> 2005 -- 06 </Td> <Td> No Angels </Td> <Td> Justyn </Td> <Td> </Td> </Tr> <Tr> <Td> 2006 </Td> <Td> EastEnders </Td> <Td> Dr. Oliver Cousins </Td> <Td> </Td> </Tr> <Tr> <Td> The Catherine Tate Show </Td> <Td> Detective Sergeant Sam Speed </Td> <Td> </Td> </Tr> <Tr> <Td> 2007 </Td> <Td> Suburban Shootout </Td> <Td> P.C.
